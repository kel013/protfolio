Tomato's Guns

Trailer

Tomato's Guns is a small team project created by 4 classmates and myself. It is a low-poly first person shooter/platformer that focuses on primarily using physics to deal damage indirectly to the enemies and smoothly moving around the map using wall jumps and sliding. 

The game primarily consists on 2 basic enemies, one ranged turret type enemy and one melee enemy that charges at the player.

I was primarily responsible for programing and designing the AI for the enemies. 

Using a state machine framework, enemies react to stimulus and transition to a new state according to their current state.

The enemies  react the player actions, such as being knocked back by the player, and will try to search for player when they are out of vision by going to the location where the player is last seen.
